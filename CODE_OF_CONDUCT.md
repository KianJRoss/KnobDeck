# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in KnobDeck a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Give clear, actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate disruption of community collaboration.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this policy.

## Scope
This policy applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests, and community discussions.

Adapted from the Contributor Covenant (v2.1).
